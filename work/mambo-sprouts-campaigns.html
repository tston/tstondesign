Mambo Sprouts Marketing E-Campaigns

E-Campaigns were designed to help a client promote an element of their business to an opt-in subscriber database. Elements can be a product, Facebook page, contest, sale, blogger campaign or any other creative idea. To promote to the audience, a proven strategy of e-blast deployments, landing page or microsite development, social media posts, blogger outreach and other elements are deployed during the life of the campaign. After a campaign wrapped, analytics were gathered and closely reviewed to make sure campaigns are always performing optimally.

Social media played a key role in promotion of these campaigns. Acting as the social media community coordinator meant creating daily content, promoting brands as well as topics that engage the target audience, answer and provide information to consumers, schedule & maintain social media calendar with Hootsuite, create & deploy relative graphics and other tasks.
