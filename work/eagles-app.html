Philadelphia Eagles App

Working with our App Developers, Yinzcam, whether it was managing an update, designing graphics, building XML feeds or working with Apple, I worked on all aspects of our App. The Eagles App is developed for iOS, Android and BlackBerry platforms. Leading the way in the NFL, the Eagles were the first team to establish an in-app purchase element for the 2013 Eagles Cheerleaders Swimsuit Calendar. The Eagles also offer extensive geo-fenced in-stadium elements, including RedZone, Tunnel Cam, Video Drives and more.
