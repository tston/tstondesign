Stranger Things Wall

At URBN IT, we take holiday decorating very seriously. Each year, the best decorated pods get awarded the prize of bragging rights for a whole year! With Strangers Things the buzz around the office, a few co-workers and myself transformed our pod into Joyce's living room from the show. My contribution was to create a way to communicate on the wall from the Upside Down World. Using a string of leds, an arduino and a simple switch case bit of code, you could type a message to the wall and have it communicate with those watching. R-U-N!
